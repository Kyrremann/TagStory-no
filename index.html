Hi. This is Tag Story.

Tag Story is an interactive, social, mobile game, designed to let you experience
the psychical environment in a new and exciting way.

What you choose dictate
the path ahead, shaping the
story as you go, letting you
arrive at unexpected places.

What

Go out and experience the wilderness.
Learn more about the surroundings you venture into.
A addition to a family outing

How

Tag Story will let you discover stories in your area. Through narrative stories you can interact with tags using your smartphone. Tags can either be physical (NFC and QR-code) or abstract (GPS), guiding you from one place to another. Experience stories by you and others.

The app

We are working on a Android app where you can download different stories. Each story will take you through a narrative with text, images, questions, and puzzles..
